Shipping

Standard delivery is 3–7 business days domestically, 7–14 internationally. Free standard shipping applies on orders over $150.

Returns

Unworn pairs in original packaging may be returned within 30 days for a full refund or store credit. Initiate a return from your order email or write us at help@scadoudle.com.

Sale items marked final are not eligible unless defective. We cover return shipping on defective products only.
